## The Problem

- **It's 3 AM.** You're having a panic attack. Your therapist is asleep.
- **Therapy costs $200/session.** Most people can't afford weekly help.
- **Stigma is real.** People want privacy and anonymous support.

## The Solution

An AI companion that provides:
- **Crisis Mode**: Guided breathing exercises for immediate relief
- **AI Chat**: CBT-based techniques, available 24/7
- **Progress Tracking**: Visualize patterns and triggers
- **Complete Privacy**: Your journey, your data
